About: Why This?

The simplest reason for why I chose this tutorial is that it was the first that came to my mind - and I figured it would be more time efficient to just go with it than to spend the time thinking of somehthing else. I was probably wrong about that to be honest, as it was a bit lengthy, but that's okay.

The reason it was on my mind at all is I have been thinking more about camping and spending time in nature lately, as I think more poeple would be happier if they engaged in these activities more often - myself included. I also think it's very cool to be able to survive without so many 'modern' tools like just kerosene and a ligheter, so I've been thinking about taking a wilderness survival class lately as well.

For those interested in learning more, I have linked the video I learned this from below. In the video, he uses a bow to start his fire instead of hand drill, but I figured that is a tutorial for a different time. Otherwise, you can just look up "bushcraft" of "wilderness survival" on Youtube and find many similar videos, even for different biomes.
